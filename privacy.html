Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 30, 2026

Назад

Word Trainer is a small English vocabulary trainer. The app is designed to work locally in your browser and does not require an account.

What Data Is Stored

The app stores your learning progress, XP, streak, selected lessons, and custom words in your browser using local storage. This data stays on your device unless you clear browser data or move it yourself.

What Data Is Sent

The app does not send your vocabulary progress or custom words to a server. GitHub Pages may process basic technical request data, such as IP address and browser information, as part of hosting the website.

Payments And Support

If you use the support link, payment processing is handled by ЮMoney at yoomoney.ru/to/4100119539185348. Word Trainer does not receive, process, or store your card, wallet, or payment credentials. ЮMoney's privacy policy and payment terms apply to the payment page.

Analytics And Ads

The current version does not include analytics trackers or advertising scripts. If analytics or ads are added later, this policy should be updated before those tools are enabled.

Children

This app is intended as a general learning tool. It does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the project owner through the GitHub repository: github.com/drag717/english-word-trainer.
